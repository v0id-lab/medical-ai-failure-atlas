# TÜSEB Route Fit Question Packet

Date: 2026 06 19

Status: public route fit question packet for a non patient data medical AI safety concept, not an application.

Purpose: convert the TÜSEB A4 UM concept gate into a short route question that can be sent to the correct TÜSEB or TÜYZE information surface without claiming submission, eligibility, funding, partnership, patient data access, clinical validation, or official role.

This packet is not a TÜSEB application, not a TBYS submission, not a TÜYZE proposal, not a partner claim, not a budget request, not a clinical deployment plan, and not a request to use patient data.

## Source basis

### TRFQ001: TÜSEB A4 UM expert call notice

Official source: https://www.tuseb.gov.tr/haberler/tuseb-2026-a4-um-uzman-mecburi-hizmet-grubuna-yonelik-proje-cagrisi-acildi-20260616

Checked fact: TÜSEB states that the 2026 A4 UM expert call was opened on 16 June 2026 to support short term projects led by medical branch specialists who are still fulfilling compulsory state service after specialty training.

Route question implication: A4 UM may be a fit only if eligibility, compulsory service state, institutional executor route, and TBYS authority are real.

### TRFQ002: TÜSEB A group project support page

Official source: https://proje-destek.tuseb.gov.tr/a-grubu-proje-destekleri

Checked fact: TÜSEB describes A4 as supporting academic work and scientific research led by academics and specialists, and says eligible project executives include personnel with doctorate or medical specialty degrees in higher education institutions, research institutes or centers, and training and research hospitals.

Route question implication: the concept should not be framed as an A4 proposal until the executor route is verified.

### TRFQ003: TÜYZE health data and AI route surface

Official source: https://tuyze.tuseb.gov.tr/kurullar/saglik-veri-organizasyonu-bilim-kurulu

Checked fact: TÜYZE public navigation lists Büyük Veri Birimi, Tıbbi Karar Destek Sistemleri Birimi, Akıllı Medikal Cihaz Teknolojileri Birimi, Sağlık Veri Organizasyonu Bilim Kurulu, Yapay Zeka Bilim Kurulu, education, reports, and projects surfaces.

Checked fact: the Sağlık Veri Organizasyonu Bilim Kurulu page states that the board provides scientific opinions and recommendations for TÜYZE institutional policy, strategy, goals, and work program.

Route question implication: TÜYZE may be the right place for a health data and AI safety route question, but this packet does not claim TÜYZE review or partnership.

### TRFQ004: TÜBİTAK open calls contrast

Official source: https://tubitak.gov.tr/tr/acik-cagrilar

Checked fact: TÜBİTAK lists 1711 YZE 2026 with application interval 15 June 2026 to 18 September 2026.

Route question implication: TÜBİTAK 1711 remains useful for smart education and consortium thinking, but TÜSEB A4 UM or TÜYZE is the more direct health route question for this concept.
