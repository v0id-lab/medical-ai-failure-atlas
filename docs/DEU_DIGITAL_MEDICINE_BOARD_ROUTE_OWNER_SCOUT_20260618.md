## Why this route matters

The public DEÜ Faculty of Medicine page describes a dedicated digital medicine board with activity areas that closely match the field gap this project is trying to fill:

1. Safe and ethical use of digital medicine applications.
2. Artificial intelligence integration, monitoring, and performance evaluation.
3. Responsible LLM use in research workflows.
4. Training for clinical researchers.
5. Data protection and ethics oversight.
6. Pre clinical validation environment discussion.

This is a stronger hospital or medical faculty route than a generic contact address because the public page already names a board, a coordinator, activity areas, and a direct contact e mail.

## Source facts

### S001: Board purpose

Official source: https://tip.deu.edu.tr/tr/dijital-tip-entegrasyon-ve-izleme-gelistirme-kurulu/

Checked fact: the page states that the board was established within Dokuz Eylül University Faculty of Medicine to support digital transformation in health services, enable artificial intelligence and advanced information technology integration, and provide guidance for effective, safe, and ethical use across the faculty.

Route meaning: this is a credible route owner surface for health AI safety readiness and clinician AI literacy.

### S002: AI monitoring and evaluation signal

Official source: https://tip.deu.edu.tr/tr/dijital-tip-entegrasyon-ve-izleme-gelistirme-kurulu/

Checked fact: the page lists artificial intelligence integration, monitoring, and performance evaluation across health fields, including real world data fit, unbiased ongoing monitoring, integration tests, data protection, ethics audit, and pre clinical validation environment.

Route meaning: the Medical AI Failure Atlas and no ranking safety report language can be framed as a pre use review and monitoring literacy surface, not a deployment or validation claim.

### S003: LLM research workflow and education signal

Official source: https://tip.deu.edu.tr/tr/dijital-tip-entegrasyon-ve-izleme-gelistirme-kurulu/

Checked fact: the page lists responsible and ethical LLM use in scientific research, including project proposal development, literature search, methodology structuring, writing, editorial processes, examples, tools, and training for clinical researchers.

Route meaning: SourceCheckup Medical, source support review, claim hygiene, and clinician AI literacy modules fit as education and research workflow readiness questions.

### S004: Contact route

Route meaning: a low risk route owner scout e mail to `digimed@deu.edu.tr` is appropriate.
