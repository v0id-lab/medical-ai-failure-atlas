## What This Is

This is clinician led medical AI evaluation infrastructure:

1. Synthetic medical AI safety cases and review workflows.
2. Failure mode analysis.
3. Source support and claim discipline.
4. Clinician AI literacy.
5. Health data quality and provenance thinking.
6. Assurance gates before any clinical use is considered.

## What This Is Not

This is not:

1. Clinical advice.
2. Patient data.
3. Clinical deployment.
4. Clinical validation.
5. A model safety proof.
6. A regulatory approval or compliance claim.
7. A Ministry, TÜYZE, TÜSEB, BİLGEM, SBSGM, university, hospital, HealthBench, MedHELM, Medmarks, MedGemma, Meditron, FDA, WHO, EU, CHAI, OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, or other institutional endorsement.
8. A compatibility, superiority, benchmark score, or official route access claim.

## Public Positioning

TR MedAI Safety Suite is a public preview of clinician led Turkish medical AI safety evaluation infrastructure connected to the Medical AI Failure Atlas.

It focuses on source support, synthetic case review, failure modes, clinician literacy, data quality, and assurance gates before any clinical use is considered.
