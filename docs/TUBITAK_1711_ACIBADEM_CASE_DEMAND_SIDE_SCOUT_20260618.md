# TÜBİTAK 1711 Acibadem CASE Demand Side Scout

Date: 2026 06 18

Status: demand side scout, not an application.

Purpose: test whether a health professional simulation and education center sees clinician AI literacy and health AI safety education as a real training need under the smart education technologies route.

This packet is not a TÜBİTAK application, not an intent declaration, not a meeting request, not a partner commitment, not a budget claim, and not a claim that Acibadem CASE, Acibadem University, SEBİT, TÜYZE, TÜBİTAK, TÜSEB, or any other organization supports this work.

## Scout verdict

The next highest leverage demand side action is to ask Acibadem CASE whether clinician AI literacy and health AI safety education could be reviewed as a simulation or education readiness problem.

Direct health AI fit remains weak for TÜBİTAK 1711 because health is not listed as an official 2026 priority area. The honest route is smart education technologies only.

## Source facts

### S001: TÜBİTAK 1711 call model

Official source: https://tubitak.gov.tr/tr/destekler/sanayi/ulusal-destek-programlari/1711-yapay-zeka-ekosistem-cagrisi

Checked fact: TÜBİTAK describes a consortium model for organizations that need AI solutions, at least one technology provider company, at least one experienced university research lab, center, public research center, or institute, and TÜBİTAK Yapay Zeka Enstitüsü.

Route meaning: a demand side scout can test whether a real education need exists, but it does not open the application gate by itself.

### S002: official priority fit

Official source: https://tubitak.gov.tr/tr/destekler/sanayi/ulusal-destek-programlari/1711-yapay-zeka-ekosistem-cagrisi

Checked fact: the listed 2026 priority areas include smart education technologies and do not list health.

Route meaning: the scout must stay in education technology framing.

### S003: Acibadem CASE public center route

Public source: https://www.acibadem.edu.tr/merkezler/case

Checked fact: Acibadem CASE is presented as an advanced medical simulation and education center with public contact route `infocase@acibadem.edu.tr`.

Route meaning: CASE is a named demand side health professional education surface, not a verified TÜBİTAK customer organization.

### S004: CASE simulation education signal

Public source: https://case.acibadem.edu.tr/tr

Checked fact: the CASE site presents simulation based education, patient safety, and medical training signals.

Route meaning: clinician AI literacy could be asked about as an education and simulation readiness problem, not as clinical deployment.

### S005: nursing simulation training signal

Public source: https://www.acibadem.edu.tr/etkinlikler/hemsirelik-egitiminde-simulasyon-uygulamalari-2026

Checked fact: Acibadem listed a 2026 nursing simulation education workshop and a separate public contact route for that workshop.

Route meaning: health professional training demand is visible, but no AI safety education need is verified yet.

Would clinician AI literacy and health AI safety education be a useful simulation or education readiness topic for Acibadem CASE to review?
