## Live source signals

### THAF001: TÜBİTAK 1711 Yapay Zekâ Ekosistem 2026 call

Official source: https://tubitak.gov.tr/tr/duyuru/1711-yapay-zeka-ekosistem-2026-yili-cagrisi-acildi

Checked fact: TÜBİTAK states that the 2026 call opened on 15 June 2026 and that 2026 priority areas are smart manufacturing systems, smart agriculture food and livestock, financial technologies, climate change and sustainability, and smart education technologies.

Checked fact: TÜBİTAK states that the model requires a consortium involving a demand side company, at least one technology provider company, and at least one experienced university research laboratory or center or public research center or institute.

Checked fact: TÜBİTAK states that consortiums are expected to meet TÜBİTAK Yapay Zekâ Enstitüsü before application, prepare a Niyet Beyan Formu, and sign a cooperation agreement covering intellectual property and commercialization.

Checked fact: TÜBİTAK states that applications are received through PRODİS from 15 June 2026 to 18 September 2026, and that preliminary registration must be completed by 14 September 2026 at 17:30.

Field read: medical AI safety is not a direct 1711 health priority in the public page. The realistic route is a smart education technology framing around clinician AI literacy, simulation, source support, and no ranking safety reporting, if a real demand side company and technology provider can be found.
