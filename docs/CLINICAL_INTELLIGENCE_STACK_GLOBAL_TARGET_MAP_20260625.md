Date: 2026 06 25

## Public Hook

## Distribution Rule

Do not cold pitch a partnership. Publish a clear artifact, then point each audience to the part that solves their technical problem.

## Audience Routes

### 1. reasoning model teams

Examples: DeepSeek style reasoning teams, open reasoning model builders

Why they care: They need verifiers beyond math and code. Clinical reasoning has state, time, missing data, and source support.

Artifact to show: Medical Reasoning Verifier v0.1

Public line: Reasoning models need clinical verifiers that track state, time, missing data, and source support.

First channel: X thread after GitHub proof is visible

### 2. health benchmark builders

Examples: HealthBench readers, MedHELM readers, AgentClinic readers

Why they care: They already moved beyond exam style tasks toward realistic rubrics, task taxonomies, and simulated clinical environments.

Artifact to show: Clinical State Language and Clinical Trajectory Engine

Public line: Medical evals need a patient state layer between a prompt and a score.

First channel: LinkedIn technical post with repo link

### 3. health foundation model developers

Examples: MedGemma and open health model developers

Why they care: Use case specific validation and adaptation need concrete state and trajectory artifacts.

Artifact to show: 20 synthetic trajectory seed rows

Public line: A developer model needs a clinical use case map before anyone can trust a smooth answer.

First channel: GitHub README and public forum friendly post

### 4. Turkiye health AI decision ecosystem

Examples: SBSGM AI route, TÜYZE, TEKNOFEST health AI teams

Why they care: Türkiye has visible AI and health AI routes. The missing public layer is clinical intelligence representation.

Artifact to show: Clinical Intelligence Stack Manifesto

Public line: Türkiye can contribute clinical intelligence infrastructure, not only health AI applications.

First channel: Turkish LinkedIn post after English technical thread

### 5. AI hardware and national technology circles

Examples: Baykar and T3 AI adjacent circles

Why they care: National AI visibility needs hard technical artifacts with health relevance, not vague calls for innovation.

Artifact to show: repo architecture diagram and manifesto

Public line: Clinical AI infrastructure is a national technology problem when it touches language, data, agents, and care workflows.
